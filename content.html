Use the input box to enter a symbol of your choice, then set the Period and Range for your chart.

You can also click the refresh icon to update your chart's data.

Access your ChartLists and all of the saved charts within

The two stacked menus you'll find above your charts allow you to access your ChartLists and all of your saved charts directly from the workbench. Once you've selected a ChartList and a saved chart within it, you can use the left and right arrow buttons to quickly flip through the symbols in that ChartList.

Save new charts to your account or update existing ones as you work

The green “Save” button above your charts lets you save a new chart to any of the ChartLists in your account.

If you're working on an existing saved chart and make any changes, you'll notice that the “Save” button turns from grey to green, indicating that changes have been made and need to be re-saved.

If you want to save those changes as a new chart instead of overwriting the original, you can use the “Save As” button.

Turn on Inspect to see interactive crosshairs and detailed price quotes right on your charts

With the Inspect tool, your cursor allows you to drill deeper into your charts. Vertical and horizontal “crosshairs” let you pinpoint specific areas on the chart and see detailed price quotes for any bar.

You can also click and drag in any direction to measure price and percentage changes or bar counts.

Set workbench preferences like chart alignment

The Workbench Preferences pop-up lets you customize various aspects of your SharpCharts experience. For instance, you can choose to center-align your charts in the middle of the screen or leave them left-aligned for a classic look.

Once you've got your charts set up just the way you like 'em, you can save those settings as a “ChartStyle”. You'll be able to quickly return to that chart template in the future for any symbol.

The ChartStyles menu here provides access to all of your saved templates, and you can also use the green “Save ChartStyle" button to the right to add a new ChartStyle to your account.

StockCharts Default

Our go-to chart setup that's perfect for any and every StockCharts user, from beginners to experts. Daily OHLC bars on a 1-year chart with three key Moving Averages, Volume, a Relative Strength line up top and the MACD at the bottom.

StockCharts Classic

Our classic, award-winning layout with daily Candlesticks on a 1-year chart. Simple yet powerful, this style includes 50 and 200-day Simple Moving Averages, Volume, RSI and MACD.

The John Murphy

A timeless, no-nonsense chart layout created by John Murphy, with OHLC Bars on a 1-year daily chart. Includes 50 and 200-day Simple Moving Averages, Volume, RSI and MACD.

Weekly Elder Bars

For longer-term analysis, this 5-year weekly chart features Elder Impulse Bars. Includes the 40-week and 156-week Simple Moving Averages, price relative to the S&P 500, and Volume. Also incorporates two popular features: the Full Quote and Price Labels.

Moving Average Focus

Used to enhance your focus on specific moving averages, this unique ChartStyle fades out the price bars to emphasize the intermediate-term 50-day and the long-term 200-day Moving Averages.

Moving Average Ribbon

A colorful collection of exponential moving averags that form a flowing ribbon, helpful for determining trend by evaluating compressions, expansions and crossovers in the shape of the ribbon.

Quarterly Moving Averages

A spin on the traditional 50- and 200-day moving average durations, this chart includes four MAs that correspond to the length of 1, 2, 3, and 4 business quarters to show a rolling average price for each.

Add technical indicators and overlays to take your chart customizations to the next level

The Chart Settings panel is where you can add dozens of technical indicators and overlays to your charts and tweak various attributes like y-axis scale, candlestick fills, dividend adjustment, pre- and post-market data, and more.

And remember, all of these indicators, overlays and other settings can be saved as a chart template for future use. That's what we call a “ChartStyle”.

Discover new charts that have been professionally designed for you with our “ChartStyle Library”

If you're looking for a great way to get up and running with SharpCharts, the “ChartStyle Library” panel has a ton to offer.

Browse the categories under the “Select Library” menu and you'll quickly find charts that serve every technical approach. Whether you're looking for specific strategies like Relative Strength or Momentum, timeframe charts for short, mid and long-term analysis, or specialized charts from our expert contributors, you'll find it here.

You can also review your own saved ChartStyles here as their own library view.
